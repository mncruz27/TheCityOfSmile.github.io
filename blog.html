Welcome to Bacolod City, a captivating blend of rich history, vibrant culture, and delectable cuisine. As you wander through its charming streets, you'll be greeted by smiling locals, each ready to share a tale about the city's famous MassKara Festival or guide you to hidden gems like The Ruins, a mesmerizing testament to Bacolod's heritage.

travel | john dela cruz San Sebastian Cathedral

Step into the heart of Bacolod City's heritage at the San Sebastian Cathedral, a timeless masterpiece that stands as a testament to the city's rich religious history. This majestic church, with its neo-gothic architecture and intricate stained glass windows, offers visitors a glimpse into Bacolod's colonial past. Whether you're an architecture enthusiast marveling at its design or a pilgrim seeking spiritual solace, the San Sebastian Cathedral invites you on a captivating journey through faith and artistry in the heart of Bacolod.

travel | mark francisco Capitol Park and Lagoon

Embrace the serene beauty of Bacolod City at the Capitol Park and Lagoon, a picturesque oasis nestled in the heart of the bustling metropolis. This verdant haven, surrounding the imposing provincial Capitol building, offers a tranquil escape for both locals and tourists alike. With its lush gardens, vibrant flora, and calming lagoon, the park provides a perfect backdrop for leisurely strolls, family picnics, or simply unwinding amidst nature's embrace, making it an essential stop for anyone seeking a peaceful retreat in Bacolod City.

travel | jess mendoza The Ruins

Prepare to be enchanted by the haunting allure of The Ruins, Bacolod City's most iconic landmark. This majestic mansion, with its crumbling yet grandiose facade, tells a tale of love and tragedy, adding a sense of mystery to its already captivating charm. As the sun sets, The Ruins transforms into a magical sight, with its silhouette against the twilight sky creating a mesmerizing ambiance, making it a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and romantics alike.

july 10, 2019
travel | lyn pearl The Negros Museum

Dive into the rich tapestry of Negros Occidental's history and culture at The Negros Museum, a treasure trove of artifacts and exhibits that bring the region's past to life. Located in the heart of Bacolod City, this museum offers a fascinating journey through time, showcasing everything from ancient relics and colonial-era artifacts to contemporary art pieces that reflect Negros' vibrant artistic heritage. With its engaging displays and immersive storytelling, The Negros Museum stands as a testament to the resilience and creativity of the Negrenses, making it a must-visit destination for history enthusiasts and curious travelers alike.

august 20, 2016
travel | tom limon NEGROS FORESTS AND ECOLOGICAL FOUNDATION,INC.

Discover the heart of Negros Island's environmental conservation at the Negros Forests and Ecological Foundation, Inc. (NFEFI), a dedicated sanctuary for the region's diverse wildlife. Nestled amidst lush greenery, this foundation is a beacon of hope for endangered species, providing a safe haven for animals like the Philippine spotted deer and Visayan warty pig. Visitors here not only witness the wonders of nature up close but also actively contribute to the preservation of Negros' unique ecological heritage, making it a must-visit destination for eco-conscious travelers passionate about wildlife conservation.

travel | john wick Bacolod Public Plaza

Step into the heart of Bacolod's social and cultural life at the Bacolod Public Plaza, a vibrant oasis in the midst of the city's hustle and bustle. This historic park, flanked by majestic trees and charming lamp posts, is not just a physical space but a testament to Bacolod's community spirit. It's a place where families gather, artists perform, and events unfold, creating a lively atmosphere that encapsulates the city's warmth and welcoming nature.

travel | tom cruz Bantug Lake Ranch

Escape the ordinary and embrace the tranquility of nature at Bantug Lake Ranch, a hidden gem near Bacolod City. Nestled amidst rolling hills and serene lakes, this picturesque ranch offers a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventure seekers. travel | john read Jojo Vito Designs Gallery

Dive into a world of creativity and craftsmanship at the Jojo Vito Designs Gallery, a haven for art enthusiasts in Bacolod City. This gallery, curated by the renowned artist Jojo Vito, showcases an exquisite collection of local artwork, ranging from intricately designed home decor to unique fashion pieces. It's not just a gallery; it's an immersive experience where visitors can appreciate the fusion of tradition and innovation, making it a must-visit destination for anyone passionate about art and design.
