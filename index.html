Trave Guide to Get In Bacolod City

To access Bacolod City, travelers can fly into Bacolod-Silay Airport, which serves domestic flights and is located about 15 kilometers away from the city center. Alternatively, they can opt for land travel using buses, vans, or private vehicles, with well-maintained roads connecting Bacolod to other cities and regions in the Philippines.

Suitable Price for Food

The average cost of food in Bacolod City varies depending on the type of dining experience you prefer. You can find budget-friendly options at local eateries and street food stalls, where a meal can cost around 50 to 150 Philippine pesos, while dining in mid-range restaurants or cafes may range from 200 to 500 Philippine pesos per person.
